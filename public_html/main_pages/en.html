Wikidata - The game!

Here, you can find a set of "games" that help to improve Wikidata, one statement at a time.

Wikidata is a complex website, and has many sophisticated functions. Many actions, however, can be anticipated by a machine, and then "just" need a human to perform or deny that action. Here, you can find many of such potential actions, and easy buttons to perform or deny them, styled as a quick game. Just click on a game below and try it out! The games will edit Wikidata for you, under your user name, via OAuth.
Remember, however, that these games do, in essence, edit Wikidata on your behalf; you are responsible for these edits.
Every game screen has a "Not sure" button; when in doubt, don't be afraid to use it!
